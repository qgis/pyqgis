class qgis.gui.QgsGeometryRubberBand¶

Bases: QgsMapCanvasItem

A rubberband class for QgsAbstractGeometry (considering curved geometries).

QgsGeometryRubberBand(mapCanvas: QgsMapCanvas, geomType: Qgis.GeometryType = Qgis.GeometryType.Line) Constructor for QgsGeometryRubberBand of the given geomType, shown in the specified mapCanvas.
