class qgis.gui.QgsAuthCertInfoDialog¶

Bases: PyQt5.QtWidgets.QDialog

Dialog wrapper for widget displaying detailed info on a certificate and its hierarchical trust chain

QgsAuthCertInfoDialog(cert: QSslCertificate, manageCertTrust: bool, parent: QWidget = None, connectionCAs: Iterable[QSslCertificate] = []) Construct a dialog displaying detailed info on a certificate and its hierarchical trust chain

Parameters:

  • cert – Certificate object

  • manageCertTrust – Whether to show widgets to manage the trust policy of certs in hierarchy

  • parent – Parent widget

  • connectionCAs –

    List of hierarchical certificates in a connection
